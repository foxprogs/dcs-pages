translationCore toolset overview

The translationCore toolset is a suite of open-licensed tools for creating, revising, translating, and checking biblical materials. This toolset enables translation teams to review their text against Gateway Language resources that are accessible in a growing number of major languages. These resources are developed by Gateway Language church networks using various tools within the translationCore toolset and serve as essential aids throughout their Scripture translation process. Our goal with the translationCore toolset is to enhance the overall quality of translations across every process step. The toolset includes:

Translation

Scribe Scripture Editor (Scribe SE)
Desktop Scripture translation editor

Scribe SE is an offline and online tool that facilitates the translation and drafting of Scripture text, audio Scripture, and foundational Bible stories, namely Open Bible Stories (OBS). The translators can access para-scriptural resources that help provide contextual accuracy to the translated text.

translationCore
Scripture checking and alignment tool

translationCore is a desktop application designed to assist the quality assurance process in Scripture translation and increase church networks’ capacity in translation skills. It enables translation teams to conduct checks in their native language, allowing them to review key theological and lexical terms and enhance the consistency and accuracy of the translated resources.

The primary users of translationCore are Gateway Language and minority language translation checkers, community language reviewers and church leaders to:

  • lead a thorough, systematic check of key terms, difficult words, concepts, names, and translation difficulties. translationCore allows the translation checkers to focus on one concept at a time in the context of an entire book. This helps ensure consistent treatment of terms throughout the Scripture text.
  • directly align a translation with the original Greek or Hebrew, providing the teams of translators with another powerful checking tool.
